  • Final Project

For my final project, I would like to make a bluetooth controlled deadbolt for use in the frame of doors and windows. My roommate likes to tape over the bolt on our door so we don't need to use keys, so I was inspired to create something that is fairly simple to install and easier to use than a traditional key. My plan is to mill the deadbolt and housing and mount it over the top of the door with a bracket. The deadbolt would work with a stepper motor that retracts and extends the bolt and an arduino to control the state of the bolt over bluetooth or internet. Depending on ease of implementation, I'm interested in creative ways to unlock the deadbolt, like a secret knock, pass phrase, or basic facial recognition. This is a vague idea and I may change my plans.
